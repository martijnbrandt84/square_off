Rules

Goal

Control the city: be the first to claim 3 of the 5 banks 🏦.

How to play

1. Players take turns.

2. Click a border between two blocks to draw it.

3. Close all four sides of a block? It's yours — and you play one more turn.

4. Does a block contain a 🏦? Then it counts toward victory.

⚡ Specials

Some blocks contain a special. Claim one and its power activates immediately.

🚓
RaidBusted! The cops got you — skip a turn.
💸
BriberyMoney talks — play an extra turn.
💣
GrenadePick a cell — all walls around it are blown up.

💡 Tips

  • Never hand your opponent a third side — they'll close the block.
  • Watch which 🏦 the opponent is close to claiming — block in time.
  • Specials can flip the game. Grab them early.
  • 💣 A grenade can rescue a nearly-closed block from your opponent.
